ANIMATION

Closing Book Animation

i did this project in my 3d modeling class. the goal was to use blendshapes to animate an object. I decided to animate a book closing. I first modeled the book open and textured this object. then I duplicated it twice and modeled one halfway closed and the other closed. i used blendshapes tool to then do the animation.

Fight Animation

i did this project in my previous animation class. the goal was to take everything we learned throughout the semester and create a fight scene. i decided to make the fight scene in a boxing ring. i first modeled the location then imported the fighters and began animating the fight. animating multiple interactions between the fighters until one of the fighters won.
